About

I am a Mexican cinematographer based in Berlin. My practice focuses on lighting design, the narrative energy of camera movement, and the creative intuition of editing.

I collaborate on fiction and documentary projects. My journey through production instilled responsibility and resilience, as well as rigor and a deep awareness of the emotional labor we carry while crafting cinema.

Available for projects in Europe and Latin America. Languages: Spanish, English, Portuguese.
